Welcome to DromitageRP!

In the start of 1997, an unexpected and chilling event unfolded, altering the course of history forever.
A mysterious zombie outbreak swept across the globe, leaving devastation in its wake.
The source of the outbreak was traced back to a leaked biological weapon, which transformed ordinary individuals into ravenous, undead creatures.
Panic ensued as the infected rapidly multiplied, leaving governments and society at large struggling to cope with the catastrophe.
Despite the valiant efforts of military forces and civilian volunteers, major cities crumbled under the onslaught of the undead.
The world was forced to adapt to this horrifying new reality, leading to the rise of survival communities and a grim, dystopian existence.
The 1997 zombie outbreak became a stark reminder of the fragility of human civilization and the potential consequences of unchecked scientific progress.

Your story begins here, but where will it end?
